Why this view

The raw log is five sensors × three gyro axes × time — too many dimensions for one static chart. This page links two views of the same data instead: the left panel is the familiar time series, the right panel takes one instant (wherever the scrubber sits) and draws it as five vectors — X/Y as an arrow, Z as a bar height. Drag the scrubber through the big rotation around sample 5–10 and watch all five arrows swing together, then settle down near sample 30+. That's the rig behaving as one rigid body, which is the whole point of this bench test.
